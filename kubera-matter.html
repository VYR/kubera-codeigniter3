KUBERA DIGITAL GOLD PLAN
Kubera Digital Gold is a unique investment platform offering secure, rewarding, and flexible gold investment options. Whether you’re looking to start small or make significant investments, Kubera Digital Gold provides unmatched benefits with 100% transparency and trust.
Key Features of the Plan
Investment Options for Everyone:
- Customers can invest amounts ranging from ₹100 to ₹10 Lakhs and beyond.
- Flexible plans cater to both short-term and long-term financial goals.
Lock-in Fixing Benefits:
- For fixing plans, a minimum investment of 10 grams is required.
- Customers opting for fixing plans receive annual interest in addition to gold price appreciation:
* 1 Year Lock-in: 9%
* 2 Years Lock-in: 11%
* 3 Years Lock-in: 13%
* 5 Years Lock-in: 15%
- Interest is credited annually, and gold price hikes during the lock-in period are passed on to the customer.
Non-Fixing Plan Benefits:
- Customers who prefer non-fixing plans earn 5% annual interest on their investment.
- Interest is calculated and provided at the time of withdrawal, ensuring flexibility for investors.
Gold Price Calculation:
- At Investment : The gold price is locked at the current market rate.
- At Withdrawal : The gold price for that day is fixed by the company.
Early Withdrawal Policy for Fixing Plans:
- If the lock-in period is broken, customers receive only the original investment amount. Interest is forfeited.
Withdrawal Options:
- Customers can withdraw their investments in:
* Gold (bars or coins)
* Cash equivalent to the investment value
* Jewelry purchased using the stored value.
Withdrawal Timeline:
- Cash Withdrawal: Processed within 72 hours.
- Gold or Jewelry: Delivered within 5-7 days.
Additional Returns with Gold Price Growth:
- Historical data shows a 10% annual growth in gold prices, and customers benefit from both market appreciation and guaranteed interest.
Investment Lease Certificate:
- Every customer receives an Investment Lease Certificate, ensuring transparency and security of their investment.
TDS & Tax Compliance:
- TDS is deducted from all returns and profits, ensuring compliance with tax regulations.
Safety & Certification:
- Gold is BIS hallmark 24-carat 999 NABL certified and managed by Vindhya Bullion, operating under RBI guidelines.
